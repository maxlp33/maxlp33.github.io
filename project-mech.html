Project Overview

This is a complete 3D model design project for a heavy battle mech. I focused on the rationality of mechanical structure and combat functionality, including weapon systems, armor details, and articulated joint design. The entire mech design considers both visual impact and practicality based on engineering principles.

The design process referenced modern military equipment and mech designs from sci-fi works, combining real mechanical engineering principles. Every component has its functional significance, from power systems to weapon configurations, all carefully planned through thoughtful design.

Technical Features

  • High-Precision Modeling ZBrush & Blender
  • Joint System Rigging Ready
  • PBR Materials Substance Painter
  • Weapon Systems Modular Design
  • Armor Details Game Optimized

Adopts a modular design concept where each component of the mech can be individually adjusted and replaced. This design approach not only facilitates later modifications but also provides greater flexibility for animation production.

Design Philosophy

The design philosophy is based on the principle of "form follows function", where every visual element has tactical significance. Heavy armor provides protection, weapon systems ensure firepower advantage, and mobile joints guarantee battlefield flexibility. This design approach ensures the mech's credibility and practicality.

Technical Achievements

Successfully achieved 3D modeling of complex mechanical structures with over 500 independent parts. Each joint is precisely calculated to ensure natural movement in animation. The material system supports various surface effects, from polished metal to weathered paint.
