Project Overview

This is a 3D recreation project based on Junkrat's grenade launcher from Overwatch, created to learn and mimic Overwatch's art style. I faithfully reproduced the in-game weapon design, including weathered metal materials, piping details, and explosive loading system. The entire modeling process focuses on detail accuracy and realistic material representation.

As a project to learn and mimic Overwatch's art style, I thoroughly studied every detail of the original design, from mechanical structure to surface wear, striving for perfect reproduction. This project demonstrates my understanding of game art and hard surface modeling capabilities.

Technical Features

  • Hard Surface Modeling Blender 3.6
  • Material Creation Substance Painter
  • Weathering Effects Procedural Textures
  • Game Optimization Low-Mid Poly
  • PBR Workflow Industry Standard

Research & Reference

Extensive reference material collection was conducted before the project began, including in-game models, concept art, and official promotional materials. Through multi-angle reference image analysis, ensured model accuracy and consistency. Also studied real-world grenade launcher mechanisms to add credibility to the design.

Challenges & Solutions

The biggest challenge was balancing the exaggerated game-style design with realistic mechanical logic. I maintained key visual features while simplifying some unnecessary complex structures, creating a final model that is both faithful to the original and suitable for production. Weathering effects in material creation also required precise control to avoid over or under-representation.
