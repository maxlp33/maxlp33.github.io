Project Overview

This is a concept design project exploring what it would look like if Rayfield, the luxury car brand from Cyberpunk 2077, made a weapon manufacturing workbench. Featuring neo-kitsch aesthetic style, I combined Rayfield's luxury design language with industrial manufacturing aesthetics to create high-end, tech-filled weapon production equipment, showcasing a vision of luxury manufacturing in 2077.

The machine design focuses on layered details and visual impact, from macro overall structure to micro surface materials, every element is carefully designed. Advanced PBR material workflow was used to ensure realistic visual effects under various lighting conditions.

Technical Features

  • Environment Modeling Blender 3.6
  • Material Creation Substance Painter
  • Lighting Design Cycles Renderer
  • Polygon Optimization Game Ready

Design Challenges

The biggest challenge was maintaining cyberpunk aesthetics while ensuring the machine's functionality and credibility. I needed to balance futuristic tech elements with practical industrial design to create manufacturing equipment that is both beautiful and logical.

Solutions

Through in-depth research of modern manufacturing and industrial design in sci-fi works, I established a design language that perfectly combines practicality with futurism. Used layered lighting and material variations to enhance machine depth and realism.
