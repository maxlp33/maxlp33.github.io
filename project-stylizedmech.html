Project Overview

This is a stylized mech design project inspired by Overwatch's art style. Features cartoon art style and exaggerated proportional design. Focuses on color coordination and visual impact, suitable for game and animation applications. The entire design process emphasizes creative expression over realism, creating a mechanical character full of personality and charm.

Design inspiration comes from mech characters in Japanese anime and games, combining elements of cuteness and power. Through simplified geometric shapes and vivid color contrasts, creates an easily recognizable and expressive visual effect.

Technical Features

  • Stylized Modeling Blender 3.6
  • Toon Shading Custom Materials
  • Color Design Vibrant Palette
  • Animation Ready Rigged & Weighted
  • Game Optimized Low Poly

Uses non-photorealistic rendering techniques to create visual effects similar to hand-drawn animation. The material system is specifically optimized for cartoon style, supporting flat shading and outline rendering, perfect for various media applications.

Art Direction

Art direction focuses on creating approachability and cuteness while maintaining the mech's sense of power. Uses rounded edges and soft curves to soften the hard lines of machinery, creating a balanced visual language. Color choices lean towards bright and saturated tones, enhancing the overall sense of vitality.

Applications

This design is particularly suitable for mobile games, animated shorts, and toy design. Its stylized characteristics make it highly adaptable across various media platforms, while being easy to produce derivative products and merchandise.
